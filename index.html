Careers

  • Web Developer
  • Data Scientist
  • Software Engineer
  • Consultant

Web developers design, build and maintain websites and website applications in line with their clients' needs.

The data scientist role has been described as “part analyst, part artist.”

A data scientist is a job title for an employee or business intelligence (BI) consultant who excels at analyzing data, particularly large amounts of data, to help a business gain a competitive edge.

A software engineer is a licensed professional engineer who is schooled and skilled in the application of engineering discipline to the creation of software. A software engineer is often confused with a programmer, but the two are vastly different disciplines.

Technology consultancies provide this expertise and help their clients use of their technology most effectively and get the best return on investment.
